DREAM RUN

About Dream Run

The NSS Unit Of SIESGST hosts DREAM RUN, a marathon for a cause that combines the thrill of running with the opportunity to make a difference in society. Participants in this marathon raise both funds and awareness for a worthwhile cause. Whether you are an experienced runner or are just getting started, taking part in the Dream Run for a Cause is a meaningful way to challenge yourself while improving the lives of others, as our motto "Miles for Smiles" suggests. The primary reason Dream Run is performed each year is to generate money and publicity for a worthwhile cause. It also serves to promote fitness and promote a healthy lifestyle in the community.

RUN 2023

The NSS Unit's flagship event, "Dream Run - Run for a Cause," is an annual marathon that brings together individuals from all walks of life to contribute towards a social cause and uplift society. This year, we are proud to support children with autism and mental illnesses by partnering with the Ramchandra Kurulkar Bahuddeshiya Shikshan Sanstha. Our aim is to raise significant funds to support the nonprofit's efforts in improving the lives of children with disabilities and autism.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Dream Run'23?

Dream Run, a marathon to promote fitness and a healthy lifestyle in the community, to raise funds and awareness for a particular cause.

What is the marathon's cause?

The cause for Dream Run'23 is Autism. The NGO is also assosiated with other activities as well such as education for mentally disabled and aadivasi

What are benefits of participating ?

Participating in the Dream Run'23 for a cause like autism can be an excellent way to make a difference in the lives of children and families affected by this disorder.

What is the registration fee?

The registration fees vary according to the kms. For e.g- 3kms- 120 Rs, 5kms- 250 Rs , 8kms- 500 Rs and if the participant wants to donate any monetary amount they can so.
